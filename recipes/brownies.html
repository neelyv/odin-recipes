Brownies

Description

Who doesn't love ooey-gooey chocolate brownies?

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour 8" pan.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter; remove from heat. Stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Beat in coca, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Spread batter in pan.
  5. Bake 20-25 minutes.
