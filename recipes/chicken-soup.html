Basic Chicken Soup

Description

A recipe for simple, wholesome, banish-that-cold chicken soup.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound uncooked chicken breast tenders, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 2 stalks chopped celery
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cans chicken broth (14 oz)
  • 4 ounces cooked pasta noodles
  • 1/2 bundle of baby spinach, rinsed and torn into pieces
  • Olive oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat and cook chicken until no longer pink.
  2. Add the chopped carrots, onions, celery, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook until carrots and onions are tender.
  3. Add chicken broth and simmer for about ten minutes.
  4. Add cooked noodles and spinach. Simmer until spinach is wilted, turn off heat, and serve.
